    Evelyn Amaya

    Interests

    I have a wide variety of interests that range from traveling to sci-fi. I enjoy taking pictures of sunsets and plants. In general, I enjoy taking pictures.
    A wise man once said, “Taking pictures is savoring life intensely, every hundredth of a second.” I enjoy watching sci-fi as well. One of my favorites is Doctor Who. I became acquainted with this very great show 3 years ago. It was like wildfire. One spark was all it took.
    Besides sci-fi, I also am very fond with the idea of traveling some day. When I say some day, I mean far into the future. Going to places far-far away. I've never really been anywhere either than Mexico, Washington D.C and Arizona. That's why my need to travel is so grand.
    Now, besides all of that - I also really enjoy technology. It's kind of ironic since at one point I was very terrified of computers. When I was 8, I used to have nightmares about them.

    My Future

    Though I honestly don't know what my future has in store for me, I know what I want. I hope to become a Software Engineer someday. And if I can't be one, I thoroughly hope I become some sort of engineer in technology. I won't stop til I've reached my goal. Though I may not be a pro at coding now, I will eventually. Besides technology in my future, I wish to study abroad. I want to travel to places around the world like South Korea, Australia, Iceland, and France. I want to make an impact on our world, whether it be small. And though the process will be very difficult, I believe I can do it. With a bit of motivation, and help from my peers I will get better. Eventually I will attain my goal and this when I know I have succeeded.
    Until then, I will struggle until I overcome and thrive.
