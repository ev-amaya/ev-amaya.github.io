I'm Still Alive, But I'm Barely Breathing

04/05/16

Hello internet! This blog post is actually going to be about my experience here in ACS:CS50! I know it has surely been quite some time since the last time I updated (and onlytime. HAHA, am I right? Alright, well that wasn't all that funny. Back to the point, I've had such a wonderful time. Sure, times have gotten tough here in this elective, and sure there have been times where I have wanted to throw in the towel (there have been days that I'm pretty sure I probably have), but that is beside the point. Was this elective at all very intimidating? Yes, very. There are times when I felt I needed a break. Did I? Of course. Was it a very long amount of unnecessary time? Absolutely! PSET 3, I completely lost my chill. I was so confused. I understood the concept, but I was given a very limited amount of practice. I watched several videos and I was thrown into a bunch of this mumbo jumbo code. I made sense of most of what was happening, but I was still somewhat confused.

I don't hate this class or any of the work that I have been doing. I love it, it's absolutely fun, but there is so much work to keep up with! My grades in other classes suffered because of excess work I was at least attempting to put into this class. Maybe know physical copy of it, but me trying to understand and watch hour long videos and trying to grasp the basics of what I am supposed to be doing. As a result, even though I blaintly tried -- I still ended up failing this class my previous quarter. Will I still continue to give this class my all? Of course. I'll try my hardest, but I also have to put so much work into all these other challenges I receive that can either make or break my classes worth 10 points.

I do want to continue to work through my computer science work because I absolutely love computer science and it is probably one of my favorite subjects, I'm just an embarassed slow learner who is too afraid to ask for help because of the response I will get.
